Architecture, in its essence, is the art and science of designing and constructing buildings and other physical structures. It goes beyond mere functionality, encompassing aesthetic and expressive qualities that reflect the culture, history, and values of a society. From the smallest dwelling to the grandest monument, architecture shapes our environment
